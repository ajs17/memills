Historical Names

1 Jan 0001
Brooktondale: from late 1700’s: Cantineville or Cantine’s Mill and a number of other variants. 1840: Mott’s Corners (often spelled Motts Corners) 1883: Brookton Feb 16, 1926: Brooktondale As a nod to Brooktondale’s history, several local businesses have adopted “Brookton” as part of their name, most notably Brookton’s Market. E. H. Mills and M. E. Mills, Mott's Corners and Brookton

1 Jan 0001
Upon moving to Mott’s Corners, Edward restarted E. H, Mills general store on his own, with his son Jonas working as clerk. Emily would eventually also work as clerk in the store, taking it over upon Edward’s death as M. E. Mills.

1 Jan 0001
Not much is known of E. H. Mills & Co. of Corfu: The E.H. Mills General store was on the NE corner of Main and Allegheny in Corfu. It was open from 1866-1880.
