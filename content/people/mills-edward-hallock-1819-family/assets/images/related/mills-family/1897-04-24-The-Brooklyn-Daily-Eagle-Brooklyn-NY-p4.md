Mrs. Sarah A. Mills, widow of the late George P. Mills, died at the home of her son, James H. Mills, at Patchogue, on Thursday after an illness extending over a period of two years. The funeral services were held to-day, the Rey. C. N. Gleason, pastor of the Patchogue Congregational church, officiating. Mrs, Mills was born at Smithtown and was 83 years old. She had been a resident of Patchogue twenty-five years and previously of Bellport, where she was a member of the Bellport Presbyterian church. Her surviving children are Charles Mills of Sayville and James H. Mills and Jesse C. Mills of Patchogue.
