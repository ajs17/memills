4. Gerorce Puitutps’ b. May 31, 1801, md. Sarah d. of Thomas Hallock Jan. 1, 1833 and had Mary A.,® Charles E.,* and George Thomas.’ Mr. George Phillips Mills was born at Smithtown, attended the district school and later Clinton Academy, Easthampton. He resided at Smithtown until 1844 when he removed to Bellport and engaged in farming. He was Supervisor of Brookhaven town from 1847 to 1851 and represented the western district of Suffolk County in the State Assembly in 1858. He died at Bellport, March 6, 1868 and is buried in the Presbyterian church-yard, Smithtown.
